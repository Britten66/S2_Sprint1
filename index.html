About Us

At The Signal Cafe we love to see people having a great time.
Whether it's to have a meal with some family or friends, have a relaxing moment to yourself, or even just to get some work done.
We offer a great selection of the world's finest brews of coffee and teas, pastries and breads from all around the world.
We also have a great menu of meals that will delight the tastebuds and leave you wanting more.
So please, come, sit, have a sip. We would love to see you!
